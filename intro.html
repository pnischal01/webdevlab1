about the site

Koenigsegg Automotive AB is a Swedish hypercar manufacturer founded in 1994 by Christian von Koenigsegg. The brand builds some of the fastest, most technologically advanced, and rarest hypercars in the world. Each car is essentially hand-built, uses radical engineering, and focuses on breaking performance boundaries.
